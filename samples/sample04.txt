.DATA
STACK S1
.LOGIC
A] WRITE(S1) (#,B)
B] SCAN RIGHT (a,C), (b,D)
C] WRITE(S1) (X,B)
D] READ(S1) (X,E)
E] SCAN RIGHT (b,D), (c,F), (#,F)
F] READ(S1) (#,G)
G] WRITE(S1) (#,H)
H] SCAN LEFT (b,H), (a,I)
I] SCAN RIGHT (a,I), (b,J)
J] WRITE(S1) (X,K)
K] SCAN RIGHT (b,J), (c,L)
L] READ(S1) (X,M)
M] SCAN RIGHT (c,L), (#,N)
N] READ(S1) (#,accept)
